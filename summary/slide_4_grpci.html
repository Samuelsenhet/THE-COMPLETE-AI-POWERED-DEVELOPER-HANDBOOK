GRPCI Mastery & The 60% Rule

The 60% Rule
Allocate 60% of your learning time to deep comprehension. Move from "how" to "why" to accelerate your path to seniority.
G
Goal
Define what you want to achieve (5% time).
R
Research
Gather sources and context (10% time).
P
Priming
Prepare your mind for the topic (5% time).
C
Comprehension
Deep dive into the core logic (60% time).
I
Implementation
Apply what you've learned (20% time).
